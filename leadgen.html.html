05 — Review Generation System

Turn Every Repair Into a 5-Star Review

85% of customers read reviews before choosing a repair shop. This system automates review collection after every repair.

The 4-Step Review System
1
Repair complete — ask verbally
"Are you happy with the repair?" — If yes: "Would you mind leaving a quick Google review? It really helps our small business."
2
Send review link via SMS (same day)
Text the customer the direct review link within 1 hour of repair completion.
3
Follow-up after 3 days if no review
One gentle reminder. Never more than 2 messages total.
4
Respond to every review within 24 hours
Both positive and negative. Shows you care. Google rewards engagement.
